Level 1: The white cross

Welcome to the first level! In this window you will find information guiding you through solving the cube.

You can close this window at any time using the red cross in the top right corner. Whenever you get stuck, simply press the "Guide" button in the top left corner to open this window again.

Solving the cube happens layer-by-layer and involves 7 steps. The first step is to create a white cross. Like this:

Before you get started making the white cross, you first need to understand move notation. A move is a 90 or 180 degree turn.

Each side of the cube gets a name:

  • U for the Up layer
  • D for the Down layer
  • L for the left layer
  • R for the right layer
  • F for the front layer
  • B for the back layer

Moving the left layer by 90 degrees clockwise is written as L, counterclockwise as L' and 180 degrees is written as L2.

Throughout the levels the cube will automatically be rotated, so to help you keep track of which face is which, each face is marked with a letter.

Now, let's get started on that white cross! The goal of this level is to make the cube look like this:

As you can see, the white pieces on top form a cross. Note that in these images, the color of gray pieces don't matter, but when a piece is colored, it does! In this case, notice how the white/red and white/blue edge pieces match up with the center pieces on the sides. This is important.

Anyway, let's make your first move! Use the buttons to move the white/red edge piece into position. A right counter-clockwise move should do the trick.
